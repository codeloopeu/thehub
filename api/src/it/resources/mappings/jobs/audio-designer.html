Ro

Audio Designer

Internship, Copenhagen
Monthly salary:

Unpaid

I'm interested
Ro is a sleep app built on the principles of ASMR using dynamic 3D audio to create a unique experience every time with a no loop policy to avoid listener’s fatigue. The people behind Ro are audio specialists and tech nerds and have years of experience in the XR industry. We have put all of our knowledge into one app to help reduce sleep onset. Currently we are part of an accelerator program and building a new iteration of the product.

We are looking for an audio designer


We would like a person who can do:

  • Sound recordings and mix in high quality
  • Research the field of ASMR (sleep)
  • Concept development on the first selection of sounds


It's a plus if you have:

  • Worked with interactive audio before (Wwise is a big plus)
  • Have knowledge about ASMR


Our expectations for you:

  • You can work independently and in a team
  • You are curious and ready to learn
  • You feel responsibility of your tasks


We offer:

  • Coffee, lunch, a nice backyard, a happy work environment
  • A startup with a huge network in the sound industry
  • A possibility to test out your audio skills and develop them
  • An opportunity to learn more about interactive audio


We are looking to hire as soon as possible.


If you want to know more about the internship you are more than welcome to contact our co-founder Per Beck Hansen on +45 28801672 and mail per@theawe.dk
